About us

At Screams.io we enable local businesses to push advertisements only when they are more relevant. Screams is a platform for marking up the adverstisement to make your consumers and business owners to advertise smartly by providing timely, and contextual ads.

We help businesses by providing them access to the most simplest advertisement platform. Screams.io started as a Whatsapp group with businesses and consumers as subscribers. Our partners include home owners, a hawker selling pani-puri to a vegetable merchant, a curry point vendor to a family restaurant, an elcetrician or a plumber , an auto ricksahw driver or a tourist cab service provider running their businesses in a locality.

We allow our partners to publish running offers, their validity and availability on our Whatsapp group. Intrested consumers could leverage the information to make smart purchases.
