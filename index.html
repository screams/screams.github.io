About Screams

Screams.io is a platform that enables business owners to publish short lived micro ads. Ads that are timely and contextual. We envision to make advertising affordable and effective. We package the latest in tech. (BLE & Mobile) to let people publish instant ads and run short lived ad. campaigns.

What do we do ?

We help businesses by providing them access to the most simplest advertisement platform. Our partners include home owners, a hawker selling pani-puri to a vegetable merchant, a curry point vendor to a family restaurant, an electrician or a plumber , an auto rickshaw driver or a tourist cab service provider running their businesses in a locality.

How It Works?

Screams is a proactive consumer engagement platform. Screams allows ads. to be more dynamic and contextual. The platform is designed to scale and meet the needs of "a hawker, a golgappa vendor,a domestic worker, a part time job seeker, a pick and carry restaurant, an organised retailer, a real estate broker".

The power of platform to push and pull out ads immediately to the designated "ad. hot spots", thus by letting businesses to be run clearance sales or truly limited period offers to attract quick customers. Screams unlike others encourages consumers to shop on the go, a travelling consumer can read an ad. from a nearby restaurant, place an order with a touch and expect the delivery at her door step by the time she reaches home.

What's Next?

Screams brings in a connected consumer model, where not only the business owner creates ads, but also the consumer. Screams lets anyone with a mobile phone to become an "ad hotspot" and lets them to serve ads.

Any one during their commute can turn on "ScreamPeerAdShare (ad. peers)" and start pushing ads. around his locality to the people around him. This brings in consumer into the business model,and ads. become more effective as they are suggested by peers.Screams lets anyone with an app to start ad. campaigns, tell neighbors about what they are good at, and make money. People can use the platform to trial run their businesses.
